# About Me

Hello, I am Agustín Baranowski, but online, I go by the handle of 0xbara. In May of 2022, I found my passion for Cyber Security, and since then, I have been continuously learning and growing in this field. 

This website is a platform for me to share my experiences and projects with the world. Here, you will find write-ups on machines, along with other projects that I have worked on in my lab. Most of my projects revolve around Cyber Security, as I am particularly interested in the various techniques and methods used to safeguard against cyber attacks.

I hope that through my work, I can inspire others to explore their interests and passions, and to never stop learning. Thank you for taking the time to visit my website, and if you have any questions or feedback, please do not hesitate to reach out to me.
